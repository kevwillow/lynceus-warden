Step 12: Argus configuration

Argus is an optional, community-maintained database of known surveillance device signatures (drones, ALPR cameras, gunshot detectors, etc.). Lynceus works without it — you'll still detect devices based on your rules and capture configuration. Loading Argus enables richer category-aware alerts.

Choose how to load the Argus watchlist below, then configure your rules-engine opt-ins. Both sections submit together; Apply on the review page will respect the rules choices once the watchlist is imported.
